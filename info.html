Bloons TD 6 is a 2018 tower defense video game and the sixth game in the Bloons TD series, developed and published by Ninja Kiwi. It was first released in June 2018 for iOS and Android. The game was later ported onto Steam, first released on Microsoft Windows in December 2018 and later on macOS in March 2020. It was later added to Apple Arcade on February 11, 2022, as Bloons TD 6+. Reviews for Bloons TD 6 are mostly positive by critics, and overwhelmingly positive ratings on Steam. Critics have noted the pre-existing success of the Bloons TD series contributing to its popularity.

Balloons

There are many types of bloons in the game ranging from the harmless red balloons to the giant BAD balloons. The bloons travel along the track at various speeds and higher class bloons can take more hits before being popped. It is important to have a strong defense in the game to stop the bloons getting through in later rounds.

Monkeys

There are many types of monkeys in the game that can have different upgrades and abilities. Not all monkeys are equal, some do more damage or shoot faster. Some monkeys known as "support monkeys" are able to slow down or completely stun bloons, delaying them from reaching the exit.
